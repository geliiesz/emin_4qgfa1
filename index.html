Why should you support the Maharlika Charity Foundation?

This charity foundation is dedicated to providing proper medical attention to the marginalized
and indigenous Filipinos. I chose to support this charity because first of all, I believe that
everyone deserves to have access to free and adequate healthcare, healthcare is difficult to get
by and not everyone can have it. Secondly, most indigenous Filipinos suffer from deformities
and difficult illnesses, through the charity's missions, facial reconstructions and treatments
can be done in order for them to live comfortably. Lastly, medical practitioners and other
Filipinos can learn to become more selfless and look for more ways to help their fellow
countrymen through these programs.
